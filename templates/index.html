Smart monitoring systems for all your vulnerable surfaces



Deploy and forget


Maginot constantly monitors all your attack surfaces, notifying you about the smallest of anomalies. So deploy your app and forget about it! Our round the clock monitoring services will make sure you know of any suspicious requests or activities aimed at your infrastructure.



Less worrying, more scaling


Maginot will monitor your infrastructure and other attack surfaces no matter the size. From small hobby projects, mid-sized SaaS to a global software service. We make sure customers of all sizes are catered to.



Detailed and concise reports


Get daily, weekly and monthly reports straight to your inbox, slack/discord channel or SMS. Maginot sorts your data into neat comprehensible graphs, diagrams and tables. Warning you of threats, entities of interest and many more metrics.

01 Social Media

Social Media and e-mail inboxes. We scan for non-ascii characters, harmful attachments, any-login attempts and more

02 SSH and network analysis

Use our open-source tools to link to our services while configuring your cloud infrastructure.
